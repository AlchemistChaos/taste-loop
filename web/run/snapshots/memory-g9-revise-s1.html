Three moves

Make the scroll work harder.

Three moves. No commercial energy required.

  1. 1

    Hook first

    Lead with the payoff. Show the product, the trick, or the before-and-after before anyone can swipe away.

  2. 2

    Talk like a person

    Swap brand-script energy for creator energy. Real face, real voice, real point. Less polish. More pull.

  3. 3

    Make the tap easy

    Close with one clear next move: shop, book, learn, or follow. No scavenger hunt. No mystery ending.
